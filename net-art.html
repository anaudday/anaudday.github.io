MEET SERA

Click the image below to open in a new tab

A short, interactive piece that follows the story of you, the main character, who meets a fellow classmate and aims to become her friend!

Some popular trends in today's internet culture include Japanese visual novels and other virtual simulator games that allow you to make the choices you want in a fictional adventure of your own. I was inspired by a lot of these story games because not only do you get to pave your own path, but much of the work goes into creating the backgrounds and characters — the art of the story — as well. This is what one could call "interactive art".
