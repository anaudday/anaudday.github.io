Nail Color Chooser

I love painting my nails. More often than not, I am indecisive on what color to paint them. This random generator was created with the p5.js editor as inspiration from my indecisiveness and my love of coloring nails. Although it originated from a very simple and straightforward idea, it still saves me from a minor hassle whenever I need it.
